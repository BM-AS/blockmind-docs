# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about BlockMind.

## Getting Started

### What is BlockMind?

BlockMind is an intelligent crypto portfolio tracking platform. It helps you:
- Track your holdings across wallets and exchanges
- Get AI-powered analysis on any token
- Monitor market conditions and trends

### Is BlockMind free?

Yes! BlockMind is currently free to use:
- Unlimited portfolio tracking
- Unlimited DeepDive analyses
- Full access to market data

We plan to introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will remain free.

### How do I create an account?

1. Go to [blockmind.app](https://blockmind.app)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email
4. Verify with the code we send you
5. Done!

No password needed — we use secure email verification.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data safe?

Yes. We take security seriously:
- All connections are encrypted (HTTPS)
- We never store your private keys
- Wallet connections are read-only
- Your portfolios are private by default

### Can BlockMind access my funds?

**No.** BlockMind only has read-only access to your public wallet addresses. We cannot:
- Move your funds
- Approve transactions
- Access your private keys

Think of it like a blockchain explorer that knows which addresses to look at.

### What data do you collect?

We collect:
- Your email (for authentication)
- Wallet addresses you connect (to track holdings)
- Usage data (to improve the product)

We don't:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your information
- Track you across the web

### Can I delete my data?

Yes. Contact support@blockmind.app to request complete data deletion.

## Portfolios & Wallets

### How do I connect a wallet?

1. Go to **Portfolio** → **Import Wallet**
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Select your wallet from the modal
4. Approve the connection

See our [full guide](/docs/getting-started/connect-wallet) for details.

### Which wallets are supported?

BlockMind supports 300+ wallets including:
- MetaMask
- Phantom
- OKX Wallet
- Coinbase Wallet
- Any WalletConnect-compatible wallet

### Which blockchains are supported?

We currently support:
- **EVM chains**: Ethereum, Base, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, BSC, Avalanche
- **Solana**

More chains coming soon.

### Why are some tokens missing?

Tokens might not appear if:
- They're very new and not in our database yet
- The balance is tiny (dust filtering)
- They're flagged as suspicious/spam

Check the "suspicious tokens" toggle to see filtered tokens.

### Why is my balance wrong?

Balance discrepancies can happen when:
- Token price data is delayed
- Token isn't in our database (shows $0)
- Wallet was recently updated (re-scan to refresh)

Try re-scanning your wallet to update.

## DeepDive

### What is DeepDive?

DeepDive is our AI-powered token analysis tool. It generates comprehensive reports covering:
- Project fundamentals
- Market data and trends
- On-chain metrics
- Technical analysis
- Risk assessment

### How accurate is DeepDive?

DeepDive synthesizes publicly available data using AI. It's:
- Good for quick research and identifying patterns
- Not a substitute for your own due diligence
- Not financial advice

Always verify important claims independently.

### How often should I run DeepDives?

Run a new DeepDive when:
- You're researching a new token
- Market conditions change significantly
- Before making a significant investment decision

Reports reflect data at time of generation.

## Market Data

### What does Fear & Greed Index mean?

The Fear & Greed Index measures market sentiment:
- **0-25**: Extreme Fear (potential buying opportunity)
- **75-100**: Extreme Greed (potential selling signal)
- **25-75**: Neutral to moderate sentiment

### What is Altcoin Season?

Altcoin Season occurs when altcoins outperform Bitcoin. Our index shows:
- **0-25**: Bitcoin Season (BTC outperforming)
- **75-100**: Altcoin Season (alts outperforming)

### Where does market data come from?

We aggregate data from multiple sources including:
- CoinGecko
- On-chain data providers
- Social sentiment trackers
- Trading volume across exchanges
