# DeepDive Analysis

DeepDive generates AI-powered reports on any crypto token in 1-3 minutes. Each report covers project fundamentals, market data, on-chain metrics, technical levels, sentiment, and risk assessment — all in one place.

## What is DeepDive?

DeepDive generates a detailed report on any token, covering:

- **Project Overview** — What the project does and its value proposition
- **Market Data** — Price, market cap, volume, and trends
- **On-Chain Metrics** — Holder distribution, whale movements, activity
- **Technical Analysis** — Support/resistance levels, momentum indicators
- **Sentiment Analysis** — Social media buzz, community activity
- **Risk Assessment** — Red flags, concerns, and things to watch
- **Summary & Action Points** — Clear takeaways and next steps

## How to Request a DeepDive

### Option 1: From the DeepDive Page
1. Go to [blockmind.app/deepdive](https://blockmind.app/deepdive)
2. Search for a token by name or symbol
3. Click **Generate Report**
4. Wait 1-3 minutes while AI analyzes the token
5. View your report

### Option 2: From Your Portfolio
1. Click any token in your portfolio
2. Click **DeepDive** button
3. Report generates with your holdings context

### Option 3: From a Watchlist
1. Open your watchlist
2. Click the **Analyze** icon on any token
3. Get insights on tokens you're researching

## Understanding Your Report

### Project Overview
The AI explains what the project does in plain English:
- Core technology and use case
- Target market and competitors
- Team background (if available)
- Recent developments

### Market Analysis
Key metrics and what they mean:
- **Market Cap** — Total value of all tokens
- **24h Volume** — Trading activity (higher = more liquid)
- **Price Change** — Recent performance
- **All-Time High/Low** — Historical range

### On-Chain Insights
Blockchain data reveals the true picture:
- **Holder Count** — Growing or shrinking community
- **Whale Concentration** — Risk of large dumps
- **Transaction Activity** — Real usage vs speculation
- **Smart Money Movements** — What sophisticated wallets are doing

### Technical Levels
Key price levels to watch:
- **Support** — Price levels where buying typically emerges
- **Resistance** — Price levels where selling typically emerges
- **Trend Direction** — Overall momentum up, down, or sideways

### Risk Assessment
Every report includes honest risk factors:
- Token concentration concerns
- Liquidity risks
- Project-specific red flags
- Market correlation risks

## Report Accuracy

### What DeepDive Does Well
- Synthesizing public data quickly
- Identifying common patterns and risks
- Providing a starting point for research
- Highlighting things you might miss

### What DeepDive Can't Do
- Predict the future
- Access insider information
- Replace your own judgment
- Guarantee accuracy (always verify)

## Tips for Better Analysis

1. **Compare multiple tokens** — Run DeepDives on competitors
2. **Check over time** — Reports reflect current state; market changes
3. **Verify claims** — Cross-reference with official sources
4. **Consider context** — A "risky" small-cap differs from "risky" blue-chip
5. **Read the risks** — Don't skip the red flags section
