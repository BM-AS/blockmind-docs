# Create Your Account

Getting started with BlockMind takes less than a minute.

## Sign Up

1. Go to [blockmind.app](https://blockmind.app)
2. Click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address
4. Verify your email with the code we send you
5. You're in!

## Create Your First Portfolio

After signing up, you'll be prompted to create your first portfolio:

1. Give your portfolio a name (e.g., "Main Holdings", "DeFi Wallet")
2. Choose how to add assets:
   - **Connect a wallet** — Automatically import all holdings
   - **Add manually** — Search and add specific tokens

## Privacy & Security

Your data is yours:
- We use **read-only** wallet connections — we can never move your funds
- Your email is only used for authentication
- We don't share your portfolio data with third parties
