# What is BlockMind?

BlockMind is a crypto portfolio tracker with AI-powered analysis. It lets you monitor holdings across wallets, analyze any token with DeepDive reports, and track market conditions like Fear & Greed and Altcoin Season.

**Core capabilities:**

- **Track holdings** across multiple wallets and chains
- **Analyze tokens** with AI-generated DeepDive reports
- **Monitor markets** with real-time sentiment indicators

## Key Features

### Portfolio Tracking
Connect your wallets and see all your crypto holdings in one place. BlockMind automatically tracks balances, prices, and performance.

### DeepDive Analysis
Use our AI-powered DeepDive feature to get comprehensive analysis on any token. Understand fundamentals, technical indicators, and market sentiment.

### Market Overview
Stay on top of market trends with our real-time market page. Discover opportunities and track the assets that matter to you.

## Who is BlockMind for?

BlockMind is designed for crypto holders who want to:

- Keep track of their portfolio without juggling multiple apps
- Understand their investments better with AI assistance
- Make informed decisions based on data, not hype

Whether you're holding Bitcoin, Ethereum, or exploring DeFi, BlockMind helps you stay organized and informed.
